About A Decor Story

Crafted from a Genuine Love for Celebration

A Decor Story was born from a simple belief: that your wedding day deserves a setting as extraordinary as the love it celebrates. Based in London, we specialise in bespoke Nikkah and wedding decoration — designing and building every piece by hand, with the kind of attention to detail that only comes from truly caring about the work.

Our journey began with a deep appreciation for the rituals and traditions woven into South Asian and British weddings — the significance of the stage, the beauty of the welcome, the warmth of a table laid with thought. We wanted to create a studio that honoured those moments, offering couples something that felt personal rather than hired-in.

We work closely with each couple from the very first conversation, taking the time to understand not just what they want the space to look like, but how they want it to feel. Every consultation is unhurried. Every design is built around the two of you — your colours, your story, your day.

London is our home, and we are proud to serve couples across the city and the surrounding counties. Whether your venue is an intimate townhouse or a grand banqueting hall, we deliver, install, and dismantle with precision — leaving you free to be fully present for every moment that matters.
