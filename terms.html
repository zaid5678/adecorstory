Terms & Conditions

Please carefully review the following terms and conditions, which apply to all bookings. By confirming your booking, you agree to adhere to these terms.

1. Security Deposit

A security deposit of £100 is requested for each booking. This deposit is returned on the same day of the event, as long as there is no damage to property or item loss. If the items are damaged or lost, the deposit will be forfeited. Please ensure you read through all of the Terms & Conditions before you proceed with your booking.

2. Full Payment

The full payment for the booking must be made by the agreed-upon date. If full payment is not received by this time, the deposit will be forfeited, and the reservation will be cancelled. In such instances, the booking will be non-refundable or transferable.

3. Venue

Please indicate the right guest arrival time so that we can come when the venue is ready for us to begin work. We request your guest's arrival time as a guideline, but the venue determines the access time and the final set-up time. If you need to set up sooner than the venue's access time, please discuss this with the venue before booking.

4. Parking

Please ensure that there is convenient and accessible parking available for us when we arrive at the location. We need to park close to properly unload and transfer large stands and equipment. This is vital for completing the setup effectively and on schedule.

Parking that is unavailable or difficult to obtain may create delays.

5. Preparation

We begin our preparations for your event 3–5 days before the scheduled date. During busy periods, preparation times may vary. Please ensure your requirements are all clarified during the booking stage.

6. Last-Minute Bookings

Events which are booked last minute (i.e. 2 weeks or less) will incur the following additional charges:

2 weeks or less — 25% premium
7 days or less — 50% premium

This is to cover the additional cost of labour and preparation necessary outside of business hours by our team to prepare for your special day.

7. Security Deposit Policy

We have noticed upon collection of our setups that ornaments are going missing and items are damaged. In light of this, we now follow a strict policy for a security deposit on each booking. This deposit will be returned on the same day of the event, as long as there are no missing or damaged items.

Please ensure you read through all of the Terms & Conditions before you proceed with your booking.
